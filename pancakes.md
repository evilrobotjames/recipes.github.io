# Pancakes

## Ingredients

- 400g plain flour
- 1tbsp sugar
- 2tsp baking powder
- 2tsp bicarb
- 3 eggs
- 400ml milk
- 1tbsp lemon juice

## Steps

1. Hob on "5", with a lump of butter
1. Mix **flour**, **sugar**, **baking powder**, **bicard**  in large bowl
1. Beat **eggs** and mix them in with the **milk** slowly
1. Add the **lemon juice** and stir through
