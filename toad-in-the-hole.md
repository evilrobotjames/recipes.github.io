# Toad in the Hole

## Ingredients

- Sausages (Lincolnshire), 700g
- Flour (plain), 200g
- Eggs, 4
- Milk, 400g
- Dijon mustard, 4tsp

## Steps

1. Oven to 200C, few tablespoons of vegetable oil in the oven dish, wait for oil to heat
2. Prick the **sausages**, in for 20mins
3. Mix **flour**, **eggs**, **milk**, and **dijon mustard**
4. Pour batter over sausages and back in for 25 mins
5. Down to 160C for 10 mins

## Notes

This is the right amount for our very largest oven dish
