# Lasagne

## Ingredients

### Bolognaise
- Beef mince, 1kg
- Carrots, 4
- Onions, 3
- Garlic, 8 cloves
- Mushrooms, 320g
- Wocestershire sauce, 60ml
- Tomato puree, 60g
- Beef stock cubes, 2
- Oregano, 4tbsp

### Bechamel sauce
- Butter, 120g
- Flour, 120g
- Milk, 1.4L
- Cheese, 200g

### Assembly 
- Lasagna sheets, 12
- Cheese for topping, 150g

## Steps
1. **Bolognaise**:  Dice everything, and slow cook everything on low (6-8h)
1. **Bechamel**:
   1. Pre-heat the **milk** to make combining quicker
   1. Melt **butter**, add **flour**
   1. Combine with **milk**
   1. Stir through **cheese**
1. Assemble and bake for 1h, 180C
